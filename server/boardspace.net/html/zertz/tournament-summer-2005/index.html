Summer 2005 Zertz Tournament

The summer tournament started with 13 players. The general format will be a preliminary round robin, round, split into two groups.  The top two finishers in each round will play quarter-final and final elimination matches.   First round matches require 3 games against each opponent.

On July 18 this was reduced to 9 players as the other 9 showed no interest in playng their games.   Attention to the tournament by the remaining players was less than perfect, players variously "forgot" about the tournament, or decided to move house and lose internet access for a while, but we did eventually arrive at a final  match.

The final  best of 7 match was played in three sessions, 6 games in all.  Congratulations to Bluedog, now officially top dog too!

You can use the Offline Game Reviewer to browse the actual games. Look in the tournament/2005/Jun/  and tournament/2005/Jul/ directories.
