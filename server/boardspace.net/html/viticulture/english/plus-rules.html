The Viticulture Plus Options



The "plus" options are a set of mix and match options, presented at the start of a game.  All players must agree on the set of options to be used
.  When an option is changed, all the player checkmarks are turned off.  The game starts when all players check ok

  • Select Green Cards from a market

    When you draw green cards, from the draw green card actions, you also draw 2 extra  cards which you can choose
    instead, and pay $1 for the benefit.  If an oracle worker is in use, one additional free choice will be available.  Gaining
    green cards from other sources (trace, stars, wakeup row, visitor cards) is unchanged.

  • Choose from 2 Mama and 2 Papa Cards

    Draw 2 mama and 2 papa cards at the beginning of the game, and select
    one mama and one papa.  This allows you to better plan a game with a
    pair that work together.

  • Unlimited Workers

    You can buy as many workers as you wish, but still only one of each type of special workers.  
    There's nothing magic about 6!  Note that several cards that mention 6 workers will mean exactly 6.
    And that various blue cards that let you train a worker can always be used.

  • Continue Playing after Entering a Season

    This is probably the most radical of the options;  when you pass to the next season, instead of waiting
    there until all the other players also enter the season, you will take your turn in the new season in your
    next normal turn.  This is somewhat like the effect of the messenger special worker - if you get to a
    seasone first, you get first choice of the placements.  Several other consequences are not quite obvious.
    • workers placed in the future by the "Planner" card will take their action in the next normal turn.
      With continuous play, there is no such thing as "first in season"
    • "Merchant" special workers can earn a card bonus if they are the first and still only worker
      in a new season.

  • Draw From Decks with Replacement

    Essentially, all the cards are drawn from the original full deck.  So there can be duplicates, and (more important)
    your odds of drawing a particular card is not changed if someone else has already drawn or played it.

  • Limit per-player visitor cards to 3 VP and $6
