Information for events at Gencon Online

(..or just show up at the appointed time, you can indicate your intentions here)

Sept 16-19, 2021

Frankly, It feels like I designed boardspace for Covid-19 times.  The site has always been a place where you can play games with friends in as close as possible to that being there feeling. Boardspace will be hosting several game play events during strategicon, which are sure to draw a crowd, but you don't need a special even or an invitation;  all the games can be played anytime  - online or offline - desktop or mobile.  Bring friends.


At Gencon Online this year, we're officially holding events for these games at these times.   Gencon will charge you $2 to sign up, but we won't be checking - whoever shows up gets to play!  And of course, the whole site is open all the time.    The general plan will be to lauch as many games as people show up for.
