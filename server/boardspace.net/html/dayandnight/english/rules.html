The Rules for Day and Night

OBJECT OF THE GAME
 
Score the most points by playing words on the grid.
 
 
SET UP

Play on any size checkerboard, with a supply of black and white stones.

Turns:
Each turn, you either
  • place a stone of your color on any unoccupied dark square
  • slide a stone of  your color to any adjacent empty light square
Winning conditions:

either:
  • form a horizontal or vertical row of 5 stones of your color
  • form a diagonal row of 4 stones of your color on light squares
