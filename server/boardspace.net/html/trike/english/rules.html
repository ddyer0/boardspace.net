The Rules for Trike


 
SET UP

Trike is played on a triangular Hex board of any size, with a single pawn and a supply of black and white stones..

First Turn:  the first player places a stone and the pawn on any cell.

Pie Rule: the second player may either make their first move, or swap colors and play the position established by the first player.

Subsequent Turns:   move the pawn any number of unobstructed spaces in any direction, place a stone of
your color and the pawn in the stopping space.

Winning conditions:  the game ends when the pawn can't be moved.  The winner is the player with the most stones under or adjacent to the pawn.
