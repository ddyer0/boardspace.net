Nineth Annual Lines of Action Tournament

  The 2004 Lines of Action tournament is to be played face-to-face rather than by email, at BoardSpace.net, during te month of December.  It's inexplicable to me, but most of the regular email players have not migrated to the realtime game, so the tournament is small, an "intimate" group of 6.  The parameters for the tournament are as follows.
  • Round Robin format, with 2 games per  opponent, one as white and one as black.
  • 30 minutes per player time limit.
  • Games to be played at any mutually agreeable time between Dec 1, 2004 and Jan 1 2005
