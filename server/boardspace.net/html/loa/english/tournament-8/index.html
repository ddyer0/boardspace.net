Eighth Annual Lines of Action Tournament
Final Round

The players in this round consisted of the winner and runner-up from each section in the first round.  For complete details of the first round of play, see the Round-1 Page.     Play in the final round started on Jan 28, 2004.  Time was limited to 10 weeks per player.   Play finally ended on May 12, 2004;  The winner is Marc Decroos (for the second year in a row), with a record of 4-1.  Claude chaunier finished second, also 4-1, his only loss was to Marc.
