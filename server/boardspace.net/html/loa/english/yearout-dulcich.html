Game Yearout-Dulcich

This game between Paul Yearout and Fabio Dulcich appeared in NostAlgia #356, P.33. , and reproduced here with permission. It's especially notable that not once during the game did either player threaten to connect!

The diagram here is of the final position, at which point Black resigned.
