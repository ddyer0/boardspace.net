Loa Tournament status

The second Email LOA tournament is complete. This year's winner is Taran, who was undefeated in the second round. Considering that this was against the winners of all four sections of the first round, that's an amazingly strong result.

This year's tournament had 28 participants, 27 humans and one robot (loa-beta). Due to the number of players, there were two rounds; the first consisting four, 7 player round-robins, and the second consisting of the winners and runners-up from the first round. Places in the first round were randomized.
