5'th Annual Email LOA Tournament, Round 1

This year's tournament had a record of 32 participants in the first round, including one strong robot player and 31 humans, ranging in skill from defending champion to novice.

Mona, the robot, is strong enough to rate as a serious threat to win the tournament; so to help the humans out, some of her games have been collected for study. You can read more about Mona and her friends at this address. 

Final Standings in Round 1

Play started on September 28, 2000.  Time limits are 1 week per move, 10 weeks overall time.  The last game finished on Jan 7, 2001.
The Winner and Runner-up from each section this round will play in the final round. Players listed in purple   are assured a slot in the final round.
