Seventh Annual Lines of Action Tournament
Final Round
This year's winner: is Marc Decroos, who finished 6-1, second place is Hartmut Thordsen who finished 5-2,.

Play in the final round started on Jan 6,2003 and was decided on April 20.   Play in the final round was extremely close; at one point  4-way tie at 5-2 looked possible, and at a slightly later point a 3-way tie for first place looked likely, as kevin, hthordsen, and mdecroos all had 5 wins, but in the end mdecroos prevailed over ragnar, giving mdecroos a 6-1 final record.  Kevin and hthordsen are both 5-2, but by winning their common game,  hthordsen finished second overall. 

Play in the first round was uneventful except that uli, the defending champion from last year, failed to qualify for the final. The full results of the first round are available on this page
