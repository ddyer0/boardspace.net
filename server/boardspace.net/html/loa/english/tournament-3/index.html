Loa Tournament status

The second round third Email LOA tournament is over, and the result is a 3-way tie for first place.

engleberg (5-2) defeated ddyer and lost to dpriddy and ottavio (4-3)

There may be some nit-picking way to decide a winner based on style points, but for my money, this is as even as it could possibly be.

Here are the results from the first round of the 1998-99 LOA email LOA tournament. The tournament has 24 participants, 22 humans and two robot (loa-beta, loa-ai). Due to the number of players, there will be two rounds; the first consisting 6 player round-robins, and the second consisting of the winners and runners-up from the first round. Places in the first round were randomized in groups of four, seeded by current standing in the server ratings.
