The Rules for Entrapment

This is my interpretation of the official rules.

The game is for two players, black and white.  
Equipment:: The board is 7x7 or 6x7 squares, with grooves between the squares to guide the placement of barriers. Each player has 3 (or 4) movable pawns called roamers, and 25 barriers.
The object of the game is to win by capturing all of your opponents roamers.

Actions:

    There are five basic actions. 
  1. Placing a roamer on any unoccupied square.
  2. Placing a barrier ressting on it's long side in any unoccupied groove.
  3. Flipping a friendly barrier from the resting position to standing on it's short side.
  4. Moving a resting friendly barrier to a new position, anywhere on the board.
  5. Moving a roamer.  Roamers can move 1 or 2 squares in any orthoginal direction, landing in an empty square.
    Roamers can jump over one friendly roamer or one resting friendly barrier, but not an enemy barrier or roamer.
    Barriers that are jumped over are flipped to the standing position, which makes them immovable and impassable to both players.

Entrapment and Capturing:    

  • A roamer which is surrounded on all four sides is entrapped.   If the roamer could not escape the entrapment square by moving, or moving a neighboring roamer, it is captured immediately.
  • If a second roamer becomes entrapped, it is captured immediately.
  • If two or more roamers become entrapped as the result of a single move, the moving player chooses which one to capture.
  • Therefore, a player can never have more than one entrapped piece.

The Play:  

  • White moves first.  Black and white alternate placing a roamer, on any unoccupied square.
  • After black places his final roamer, white can either move a roamer or place a barrier.
  • For the remainder of the game, players alternate taking two actions per turn. 
    The first action must be to move a roamer.  If a roamer is currently entrapped, it must be moved.
    The second action may be either to move a roamer or to place a barrier.
    • If the roamer moved on the first action was entrapped, and it is still entrapped, no other roamer may be moved.
    • If no unplaced barriers are available, you can move any resting barrier to a new position..
