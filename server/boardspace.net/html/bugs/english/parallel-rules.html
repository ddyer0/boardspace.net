The Rules for Bugspiel

OBJECT OF THE GAME
 
Score the most points by playing bugs and bonus cards.    

Components
  • Bug Cards, each card represents a species of invertibrate. 
  • Bonus cards are associated with 1 or 2 groups of species.
  • Hexagonal "Terrain" Tiles of  4 types: Aquatic, Soil, Grassland or Forest
  • Victory Points are the only currency in the game.  Buying cards costs victory points,
    and playing cards gain victory points.
  • The market holds some number of bug and bonus cards at increasing prices.
 Set Up:
  • A few random categories of bugs are selected, enough to make a bug deck.  All the bug cards from those categorties are shuffled together to create the bug deck.
  • The bonus deck is created using proportional numbers of bonuses for each category of bug.
  • Each player gets 1 random low value bug and 1 random bonus card and 100 victory points.
  • Board tiles are selected in a propotion appropriate for the habitats of the bugs in play, and arranged randomly to form a board.
Rounds:
The game is played over a number of rounds.   Each round in the game has 3 phases.
  • Purchase.  In this phase all players simultaneously and secretly purchase as many bug and bonus cards
    as they wish to from the set that is offered.  There are no restrictions on the number or type you buy.
    When all players have finished purchasing, the purchases are revealed.  Duplicate cards are issued if appropriate.
    The cheapest cards in the market, and all cards that were purchases by any player, are removed from the market.
    All the remaining cards are moved down to cheaper positions, and the empty slots are refilled from the decks.
  • Play Or Move Bugs  In this phase, players take turns either playing a new bug or moving an already played bug,
    subject to the placement rules.  Newly placed bugs score the point value of the bug.  Moved bugs do
    not score.   Bugs may be moved only once per round.   When all players have played or moved as many
    bugs as they wish, the next phase begins.
  • Play Bonuses In this phase, all players can play "cash in" as many bonus cards as they wish.  When all
    players are finished, the next round begins.
End of Game:

    the game ends at the end of a round when no new bugs were played in the "Play" phase, or when there are vacant slots
    in the market,  or when a specified number of points is scored (default 300), or when a specified number of rounds have
    been played (default 10)..
