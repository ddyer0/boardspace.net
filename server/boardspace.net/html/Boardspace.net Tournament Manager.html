Rapid Hive Tournament rules    (12th December 2020)

  • In order to play you need a Boardspace account (http://boardspace.net/english/register.shtml) and you have to sign up for this tournament on this page.
  • In order to play you need a Discord account (https://discord.com/) and join the Hive server here:https://discord.gg/U5zCSNHANQ. All communications about pairings, standings and anything else will be done here.
  • Registrations end on December 12th at 12noon GMT (1 hour before tournament starts).
  • Round 1 will start at 1pm GMT of the same day.
  • Please be on Discord at least 15 minutes before the start of the tournament to confirm you are taking part of it.
  • The tournament is made of two phases:
  1. a Swiss-phase, 6 rounds (no knock-out). Tie-breakers are, in order: 1. Bucholz cut 1; 2. Bucholz.
  2. The top 2 players at the end of the previous phase will play a final match made of 2 games for gold and silver medals. Top players 3 and 4 will play the same kind of match for the bronze medal. The colours are picked randomly for the first game and then swapped in the second game. In case of a tie, a single armageddon game will be played (+1min difference time control).
  • To start a game, pick any free room on Boardspace, click on the name of the game and select from the list ‘Hive’ → ‘Hive-PLM’. Then click on ‘Player 1’ if you play white or ‘Player 2’ if you play black. Click on ‘Select Robot’ and select ‘No robot, Player 1 moves first’. Then click on ‘Please, Join me’ and select ‘Tournament game’. Your opponent will click on the other remaining player and the game will start.
  • Seeding for the first round will be determined via BGA Elo. At the moment of the registration or anyway before the start of the tournament, players must provide their most recent BGA Elo ratings and with their BGA account name filling the spreadsheet at the following link: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1rFVf9mQ4jrMgK-01c02gGBjPf_8RiRBeBxOnQki1rRw/edit?usp=sharing
    New players without a BGA account will be given an Elo rating of 1.
  • Time control is 10 minutes per player with no extra time. As soon as your own time exceeds 10 minutes you MUST resign. If your opponent goes over time and keeps playing you should resign and tell a member of the committee what happened. The game with time log can be reviewed and the controversy resolved.
  • All games should be played using all the official expansions (Hive PLM).
  • All communications will be done through the Discord server. The committee will post in this chat room the link to pairings of the next round. After every game ends, both players MUST report the result of the game in the channel ‘results submission’.
  • If anything is unclear, use the Discord channel for questions or contact Frasco, jKro or Quodlibet directly.
