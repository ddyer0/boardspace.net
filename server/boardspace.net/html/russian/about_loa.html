Lines of Action

Lines of Action is the original connection game.  The players start with their pieces in two groups on opposite sides of the board, with the goal of uniting all the pieces into one group.  The pieces must always move exactly as many spaces as there are pieces of either color anywhere along the line of movement.  These rules define a very chess-like game nicely balanced between strategy and tactics.

As a fairly old and relatively well known game, there are many variations of LOA, and one unique variation called LOAPS is played here.  LOAPS was originally developed as challenge for a programming contest.
