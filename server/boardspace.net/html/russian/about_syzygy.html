Syzygy

Syzygy is a close relative of Cookie Disco, with similar movement and placement rules, except that the goal is three connected planets of the same color in a row (which astronomers call a syzygy).

You can read the details in the official rules, but briefly; planets have to be slid around the perimeter without disturbing any other planets or separating the universe into two groups.  The distance a planet can move depends on the planets it touches.  Red planets are 4, Blue Planets are 3, Green Planets are 2 and Yellow Planets are 1.   The winning move forms three connected planets in a row of the same color.

Robots:  Dumbot is a beatable, but will not miss an easy win.
