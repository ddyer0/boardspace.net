Blooms

Blooms is essentaially Go, played on a hex grid, with two colors per player, with rules crafted to be simple and unambiguous.   The key concepts, similar to Go, are groups and liberties, but with two colors per player, there are very interesting tactical twists.  

Read the complete rules here.
