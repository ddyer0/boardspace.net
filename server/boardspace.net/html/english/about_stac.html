Stac

Stack is a very simple game, with some similarities to Santorini.   The board starts filled with disks, and the goal of the game is to construct and claim 4 stacks of 3 disks.  It's really simple, plays very fast, and not too much of a brain burner. 

Robots:  To be determined, but adequate to show how to play.
