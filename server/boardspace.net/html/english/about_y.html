Game of Y

The Game of Y is  a connection game played on a unique 3 sided board, with the objective of connecting all three sides of the board.   As with Hex, when the board is full there is guaranteed to be a winner.

Y can be played on a variety of roughly triangular boards with Hex (6 way) connectivity, but the most unusual and interesting is the curved board (available from Kadon Enterprises)  which has 3 points with only 5 neighbors.
 


Robots:  weak.
