Kulami


 Kulami is an area control game, where the board is constructed from a random arrangement of 17 rectangular tiles.  Scoring is based on the sum of the sizes of all the tiles you control at the end of the game.  After the first turn, players alternate playing a new marble in the same row or column as the previous move,  and not in the same tile as in the two previous moves.
