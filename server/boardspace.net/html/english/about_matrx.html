Matrx Gipf

Gipf was the first in the series and subsequent games (Tamsk, Zertz, Yinsh, Dvonn,  Punct) were delivered with mysterious additional pieces called "Potentials".  The future story for these pieces was that they'd be used in a meta-game that linked all the games in the series.    That never quite materialized, but here we have a new game which combines the basic ideas and mechanics from Gipf, with a larger board and new pieces.
