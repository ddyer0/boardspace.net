The Manhattan Project

The Manhattan Prohect is a medium-high complexity worker placement game, loosely based on building atomic bombs.   Don't worry: they're built and stockpiled, not dropped on cities. In the game, you mine yellowcake, enrich it to bomb grade uranium or use reactors to create Plutonium.   The resulting fissionable material is built into bombs which can be tested or loaded onto planes.

The "nations" expansion adds national capabilities which somewhat mirror their namesake's capabilities, and the "personalities" expansion adds mentors with powers related to their historically accurate activities.

Read the rules and notes on the implementation.

Robots:  Not terrible (without tuning) so maybe eventually quite good.
