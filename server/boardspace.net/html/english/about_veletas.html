Veletas

Veletas is an interesting territory game.   The goal of the game is to claim mobile pieces called shooters by immobilizing them.  The twist is that the new owner of an immobilized shooter is the owner of the largest orthogonally adjacent group of stones.

The full sized game is played on a 10x10 board, but shorter games with fewer shooters can be played on 9x9 or 7x7 boards.


 Robots: Look pretty competent, exact playing strength to be determined.
