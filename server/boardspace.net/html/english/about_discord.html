Boardspace Notifications

 

When participating in tournaments or turn based games, boardspace will send messages relevant to events.  We all know that emails are unreliable - they get lost in the clutter, filtered into spam boxes, blocked by providers and so on.   The notifications address in your profile is to help you to not miss events relevant to the games you're paricipating in.   The "notifications" address in your user
profile can be one of three things:
  1. Blank.  In that case, your registered email address is used to send notifications
  2. An email address.  In that case this address is used instead of your regular email address for notifications.
  3. A discord UID. In that case the message log on the boardspace discord server will also generate an @mention which you can cause to generate an immediate notification.
  4. A uid from Alertzy.

About discord.

Discord is a free service that provides interactive text,  images, audio and video.  It's good for real time chats, and not bad for blog-like chatter.  It's widely used in the game community.
Apps are available for all the common platforms, and they all share the same content.  Boardspace encourages everyone to set up a discord account, install the apps on your devices.

Boardspace has a discord server which you should join.  Here's the invite.

I recommend using discord
  • as an alternative to Zoom to accompany real time games among friends.  
  • to meet, greet and monitor opponents in tournament games.  
  • for general chatter about the site or playing on it.
One of the channels on the boardspace discord server is the "tournament-notifications" channel., which logs activity in boardspace tournaments.  It's probably too noisy to watch all the time, but if you jump through a few hoops, it's possible to get a realtime notification on all your discord apps whenever a tournament e-mail is sent to you.  The hoops are:
  • find your discord user id (it's an 18 digit number).  It's currently difficult to find on discord but here are the instructions.
  • edit your boardspace profile and add the ID to it.
  • in the boardspace tournament-notifications channel, set the notification-settings to @mentions only

About Alertzy

Alertzy is a standalone app (ios or android) whose sole purpose is to receive notifications.   It's free at the scale you're likely
to need for game or tournament notifications.  Simply install the app on your favorite mobile, and use the UID for boardspace notifications.

Email Tricks

Depending on how complex your email habits are, you could an email address for notifications which could be
different from your usual flow, and more likely to catch your attention.  For example, if you use a protonmail.com email address
for this (and not much else) then the protonmail  apps' "you have mail" notifications will be sepatate.

Another possibility is to turn emails directly into notifications, similar to Alertz..  To do this, install the apps (or browser extensions)
from pushover.net. or pushsafer.com  These apps exist solely to receive notifications that you send them.  Then, use the email address
they provide as the notification address.  These services aren't quite free, but they're very inexpensive.
