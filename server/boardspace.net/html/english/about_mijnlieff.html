Mijnlieff

Mijnlieff is a perfect "small" abstract.  Played on a 4x4 board, each game is 16 moves or less.  It's also (in my mind anyway) a sort of logical dual of Quarto.   Each play determines where the next tile can be played.  What could be simpler?

Read the official rules, it will take about a minute.

Robots:  For such a a short and simple game, I expected the robots to crush, but it's not the case; the're quite beatable.
