Traboulet

Traboulet (also known as Kuba and Akiba) is a pushing game, with a clever mechanical construction which enforces the movement rules.   It looks superficially like Abalone, except on a square grid, but it is a completely independantly invented game.  The rules are here.

Robots:  The robots are very basic, but seem to scale pretty well.  Dumbot is not quite a pushover, bestbot appears to be pretty strong.
