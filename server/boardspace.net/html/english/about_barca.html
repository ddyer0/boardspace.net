Barca

Barca is a board game based on the paper-rock-scissors mechanic: Mice are afraid of Lions, Lions are afraid of Elephants, and Elephants are afraid of Mice.  The race is to occupy 3 of four "watering holes" in the center of the board, but you must avoid being adjacent to any scary animals that are not your own color.
