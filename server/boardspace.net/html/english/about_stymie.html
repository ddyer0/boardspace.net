Stymie

Stymie is a combined capture and area control game.  Starting with an empty board, capture seven opponent pieces, or get seven of your pieces into the center area.  The rules are pretty simple, but interestging enough to present a lot of tactical considerations.

Robots:  Pretty good - you'll need practice to win.
