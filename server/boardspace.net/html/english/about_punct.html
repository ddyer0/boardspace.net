Pünct

Pünct is a connection game which includes some 3D elements.  The primary goal is to form a chain connecting two opposite edges of the board, but the rules allow the pieces to be placed, moved, or stacked in various ways.

Pünct is probably the most brain-burning of the games at boardspace.

Robots:  the robots are good enough to win against beginners,  but serious players will probably not be challenged.
