Trench

Trench is a capturing game with mechanics inspired by trench warfare during world war one.    The two sides are divided by a shared "trench" along the diagonal, and there are interesting, asymetric rules for attacking to, from and within the trench.  As is appropriate to the theme, it's a war of attrition; the goal is to decimate your opponent rather than an elegant checkmate as in chess.

Read the official rules.

Robots:  Officially described as "challenging".  Lower ranked bots just count the wood, but smartbot and bestbot consider more.
