Push Fight

Push Fight™  was created in 1988 by  Brett Picotte.

Push fight is a brilliant abstract with few rules and lots of nuances.  The essence is that you manouver your pieces and attempt to push one of your opponent's pieces off the board.  That's it!  It plays very quickly, but has good depth.

You can read the details in the official rules here.

Robots:  Still preliminary, but the bot seems competant but not unbeatable.
