Android and IOS

The apps ought to work from any modern device, but tablet-sized devices with at least 1G of memory are recommended.   Phones will work, but the screens are annoyingly small. The CPUs, of mobile devices are pretty wimpy, so the robots for many of games are noticeably weaker.

The apps do not have any advertising or in-app purchases.  Just the games.
